The Mondstadt Nation is located in the northeastern part of Teyvat. In its lands one can see both wide verdant plains and craggy mountains, landscapes both embellished by the presence of dandelions, a gift from the Archon of Anemo: these are also the symbol of Mondstadt, since its seeds can ride free on air currents.

The Order of the Knights of Favonius is the entity that controls the Nation, at whose head is currently Grand Master Regent Jean. The knights are tasked with protecting Mondstadt, defending its ideal of freedom and maintaining order among its inhabitants.

Our library

The Mondstadt Library was once the largest library in the northern half of Teyvat. However, after most of the library burned down during the Great Fire of Fall Equinox at an unknown point in the past, it was reduced to one-sixth of its original size.
